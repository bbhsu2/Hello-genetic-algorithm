Hello World Genetic Algorithm

Target: Hello World!

This algorithm starts by populating 2,048 12-character words to a list. Each character is randomly generated. Then, a fitness calculation is done on each word, and the list is sorted in ascending order-- the lowest fitness is the citizen most like the 12-character sequence "Hello world!"


The top 15% of citizens in terms of fitness are unchanged, and the other 85% are randomly mated with each other, making new citizens. Then, 25% of all citizens are randomly mutated by 1 character. This new population is sorted by fitness, and the algorithm continues to run.


The eventual end result is 12-character sequence that reads "Hello world!"
